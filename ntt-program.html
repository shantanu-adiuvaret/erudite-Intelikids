Nursery Teachers Training Program

Empowering Innovation, Delivering Excellence

At Erudite Preschool, we are proud to collaborate with the Adiuvaret Research & Education Foundation (AREF), an organization recognized by the Government of India, dedicated to advancing research and education in child development. Through this partnership, and in collaboration with D Y Patil University, we offer comprehensive 6-month training programs and 1-year certificate courses in Nursery Teachers Training (NTT).

Our program is specifically designed to empower early childhood educators in India, equipping them with innovative methods and skills to enhance their teaching practices. This collaboration ensures that teachers are well-prepared to create a positive and enriching learning environment, fostering the growth and development of young children. At Erudite Preschool, we are committed to raising the standards of early childhood education by offering our educators world-class training and development opportunities.

  • Online Live Classes
  • Additional Udemy Certified
  • AREF and GBSRC Certificate
  • Placement Assistance
