Day Care

A Safe and Nurturing Environment for Your Child

We understand that daycare/childcare services within our preschool can be a vital resource for families. We provide a warm and family-oriented environment where the child is cared for, observed, and encouraged, allowing parents to have a stress-free time at work.

By combining preschool education with daycare, we offer children opportunities for structured learning, social interaction, and play. Parents can be reassured knowing their children are cared for in a safe, secure, and well-monitored setting, while having a sense of belonging.

Our daycare service will provide:

  • Flexible Timings, Age-Appropriate Activities, Separate programs tailored for young toddlers, and kindergarteners
  • Trained caregivers and educators to ensure professional yet loving care
  • Cozy, child-friendly, and safe spaces equipped for various activities
  • A clean environment and provision of nutritious meals or snacks with a research-based checklist for proper nutrition and hydration
  • Parent Communication with regular updates on child’s activities, milestones, and well-being
