Erudite InteliKids Preschool - FAQ

Below are some of the most frequently asked questions about our preschool, including admissions, curriculum, and policies.

1. What age group do you accept for admission?

We accept children from the age of 2 years to 6 years for different preschool programs.

2. What is the admission process?

The admission process includes filling out an application form, a school tour, and an interaction session with the child and parents.

3. What curriculum do you follow?

We follow a play-based, activity-driven curriculum designed to enhance cognitive, social, and motor skills in early childhood development.

4. What are the school timings?

The school operates from 9:00 AM to 12:30 PM for regular preschool programs and extended daycare services up to 6:00 PM.

5. Do you provide meals and snacks?

Yes, we offer nutritious meals and snacks, prepared keeping in mind the dietary needs of young children.

6. Is transportation available?

Yes, we provide safe and secure transportation services with GPS tracking for parents' convenience.

7. How do you ensure child safety?

We have CCTV surveillance, trained staff, and child-friendly infrastructure to ensure the safety of every child.

8. How do you handle emergencies?

We have a well-equipped medical facility and trained staff to handle any emergencies. Parents are notified immediately in case of any concerns.
