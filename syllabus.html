Syllabus

Good quality preschool syllabus have been found to have a positive impact on children becoming interested in lifelong learning. Children pass through an identifiable sequence of developmental milestones in different domains. Accordingly, syllabus requires careful planning for a stimulating and enriching environment so as to develop their full potential.

Our activities are designed to ignite curiosity, creativity, and confidence in every child. The syllabus is designed as per the suggestions given by ECCE and NCERT, Govt. of India in its new education policy for the pre-primary level of education and skill building.

All the above skill components are taken into account and progressively put into syllabus for Play Group (2-3 years), Nursery (3-4 years), LKG (4-5 years), and UKG (5-6 years).
