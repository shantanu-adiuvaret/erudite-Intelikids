Art Program

Fostering Creativity and Imagination

At Erudite Intelikids, we believe in the importance of nurturing creativity and artistic expression from an early age. Our Art Program is designed to provide young children with the opportunity to explore their creativity through various art forms. Through guided activities and hands-on projects, children will develop their artistic skills and express themselves in unique and imaginative ways.

  • Drawing and Painting
  • Craft and Collage
  • Clay Modeling
  • Photography Basics
