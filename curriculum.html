Our Curriculum

A Holistic Curriculum Blending Montessori, STEM, and Creative Learning for Early Childhood Development.

The National Policy on Early Childhood Care and Education (ECCE) 2013 Visualized

"Nurturance and promotion of holistic development and active learning of all children below 6 years of age by promoting free, universal, inclusive, equitable, joyful, and contextualized opportunities." The early years are when the child's brain development is at its fastest pace. Communication skills and social/personal habits are also forming at the same time. Hence, supported by a team of experts, our curriculum ensures laying a foundation for the child's all-round development.

We follow a 5*4 skill-based curriculum:

Language and Communication Skill

Cognitive Skill

Social and Emotional Skill

Environmental Skill

Physical and Movement Skill

This curriculum is enriching and innovative in nature, designed to promote holistic development and active learning in children.
